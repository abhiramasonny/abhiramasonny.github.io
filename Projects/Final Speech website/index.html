Our Purpose

Spech and Debate is a club with MUCH more than one reason to join. Here's just a few:

For the Learning

Our directors and mentors have all received high quality instruction that has curated each of their individual competitive experiences and strategic perspectives. Each practice speech, spar round, and tournament has allowed the mentors to perfect their skills against hundreds of people striving for the same goal, and they are willing to share their passion and impart their knowledge to you in an effective, yet appealing fashion.

For the Future

Speech and Debate will teach you the basic skills of logicality, extemporaneous speaking, critical thinking. etc. These skills will allow you to reach your goals easily and efficiently. By achieving the small goals in life, you can move on and implement your learned skills to help you get into better colleges and higher paying jobs. Speech and debate will teach you to control your emotions in an argumentative field, and allow you to perceive matters in a more logical and critical fashion.

For the Fun

Speech and Debate club is much more than just a group of nerds battling it out with some over-complicated vocabulary. It's a place where you can hang out with friends and share a few laughs in a casual environment, all the while learning how to solve life's problems a little better. With us, you will make lifelong friends and unforgettable memories.
