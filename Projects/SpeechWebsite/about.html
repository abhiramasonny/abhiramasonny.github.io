Anton Joseph

Impromptu/Extemporaneous Director

Hi, my name is Anton Joseph, and I am an 8th grader at Ereckson Middle School. This year, I am an officer in Speech and Debate, and I am so thrilled to be able to. There are many reasons why I chose to teach in this club, but the main reason is my passion for speaking. Speaking has always been something that has come naturally to me. Whether it was arguing with my brother, or participating in UIL, I never doubted my ability to speak, and convey messages. I don’t consider myself the best at speaking, but I do feel that I have an above average speaking ability. I did my first bit of public speaking when I was in 3rd grade. All students were tasked to write a speech for Veterans Day, and I was told to speak mine aloud in front of our school since mine was the best in the grade. After that, I did impromptu speaking for UIL in our school district along with some of the members in this club. In the final competition however, no other schools participated, making me and my club mates have to face each other for the UIL. In the end I placed 2nd out of 3 people. Yeah, not an amazing achievement, but I’m still proud of it. I haven't professionally done speaking in tournaments and competitions, and I haven’t attended classes for it. Everything I know was self-taught, but I don’t consider that a disadvantage. Even with my lack of education, I’m still able to keep up with the best speakers in our school with speeches for classes and other activities. To be honest, if my resume for public speaking was compared to others in the club, it would be ranked dead last. But it's okay. I know that so many kids coming into this club have no formal experience, and some aren’t self-taught. This makes teaching them all the more fun and better, as I can relate to new members more and more. Along with Speech Debate, other clubs I'm doing this year include BEST Robotics and TMSCA. Both of these clubs I did last year as well. For the people who actually read through my insanely long bio,(sry for it btw) I do want to tell one last thing. When I was told to write this bio, I procrastinated A LOT. Because of that I owe some of my club members boba, lol. When I started writing, I decided that I wasn’t gonna make myself look like a Grade A, future valedictorian student, because I'm not. I told the truth, maybe even dumbed it down a little, so that all of you who read this know that you don’t have to be a valedictorian to join this club. You don’t need to go to Harvard in the future, have 100s in all your classes, or even have past experience in Speech and Debate. You can join for whatever reason you want. And we will still have fun, either which way.

Contact me at anton.joseph@student.allenisd.org
