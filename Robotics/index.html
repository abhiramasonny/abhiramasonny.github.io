Team Makeup


We are a rookie FTC Team, with this being our first season in FTC, and the first time many of our members have participated in robotics overall. Being a High School Robotics Team, we consist of mostly freshmen and have 3 sophomore mentors to guide us along the way. When it comes to sustainability, our team ensures that we market our club to the younger generation, hoping that they will be interested in joining FTC Robotics once they are in High School.

About Our Team

Vortex Robotics is a FIRSTÂ® Tech Challenge (FTC) Robotics Team from Lowery Freshman Center in Allen, Texas.
